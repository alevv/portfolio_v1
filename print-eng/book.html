AVV, Experimenting With Typography

The book AVV Experimenting With Typography looks at different creative uses of typography and book arts to create a fun experience for the reader. The project inside the book Nature and Letters utilizes food to create type that relates to the materials used and their form. The project Visual Sequencing Implements the idea of icon, index and symbol to tell a story in a sequence, while the book itself reveals its content dynamically.
