Who are we?

Young Creators is here to engage kids into STEAM (Science / Technology / Engineering / Art / Math) activities, and helping parents understand the benefits and how to approach tech literacy.


Parents of the Young Creators

Tech is one of the fastest growing industries. Kids deserve the opportunity to broaden their experience in the field of technology before they enter college. Engage the youth in tech-literacy by teaching fundamental skills such as coding, design, and critical thinking.
