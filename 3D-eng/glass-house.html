TOOLS: AutoCAD + Illustrator + Photoshop // Type of Project:  Furniture design & technical illustration // Deliverables: Renderings, technical illustrations & 3D prints

Actual to size, created to be built

This line of luxury furniture was inspired by the geometry, light and architecture of the house that holds it. The use of glass and metal grant the pieces a unique look and the technical illustrations provide real information about the sizing and materials used to construct the line.







The inspiration = a project itself

The house itself was created in AutoCAD. It draws inspiration from the basic form of a square topped with a triangle and explores the beauty of an exposed structure, angled roofing and the transparency granted by glass. The house then inspired the line of furniture that came to populate it.



It's an experience!

A panoramic rendering allows for an immersive experience of the Glass House through a VR headset or the simple use of a smart phone. This gives the viewer a sense of the scale of the objects and lends a more realistic interpretation of the design.
