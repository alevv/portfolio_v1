Vessel The Guild of Bookworkers

Approximately 600 visitors flooded the Kellogg Art Gallery to enjoy the Vessel the Guild of Book Workers exhibition. This show had existing branding which I extended to create the collateral material along with wayfinding and wall pieces to complement the show’s aesthetics. The wall pieces were made from the covers of donated books and enriched the visitors’ experience, adding an environmental design component to the project.

The collateral included:

  • Vynil title walls
  • Didactic panels
  • Outdoor signage
  • Tabloid posters
  • Postcards
  • Instagram squares
  • Facebook covers
  • Electronic billboards
  • E-Blast
