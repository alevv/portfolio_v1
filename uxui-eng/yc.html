Young Creators

Young Creators is an organization focused on engaging kids in STEAM(Science Technology Engineering Art Math) activities and guiding parents in their approach toward tech literacy. The website, youngcreators.org, features easily accessible information regarding tech literacy. The Useful Notebook is an activity and coupon booklet, funded through the advertisements it contains, which would be distributed for free in several CA school districts to attract both kids and their parents to Young Creators.

The Logo // Style Guide

The Young Creators logo aims to represent STEAM while obviously expressing its intention to cater to children. Elements representative of STEAM include the gear, symbolizing innovation brought about by tech literacy, wireless signal bars, signifying modern technology, and atom rings, representing science. The color pallet and chosen type, Avenir Next Heavy, express the fun and creativity meant to draw in children and excite them about tech literacy.

The Useful Notebook

The purpose of the Useful Notebook is to deliver information about Young Creators to kids and their parents. It would be distributed for free to families through the schools in several districts. Advertisements featured in the notebook would be used to fund its production. To invite parents and children to interact with the notebook it would also contain coupons as well as puzzles, such as sudoku, and notes and groceries pages.
